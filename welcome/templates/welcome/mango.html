Mango

5x more vitamin C than oranges, good source of fiber.

  • Purchase - aroma is best indicator of ripeness, fruit should have distinctive smell without any hint of ammonia (signal that it is overripe), and a slight give when squeezed. Continues to ripen at room temperature after harvest.
  • Storage - once ripe store in crisper drawer of fridge to prevent over-ripening.
