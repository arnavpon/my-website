Beets

High in boron, which elevates testosterone markedly & vasodilates; low glycemic index, extremely high levels of antioxidants; can increase athletic performance. Red color comes from betalain, which has anti-tumor properties.

  • Purchase - purchase the darkest red/purple beets, preferably with leaves on (fresher + higher nutrition).
  • Storage - store leaves in microperforated bag, separate from roots, eat within 2 days.
  • Preparation - cook beats by steaming or roasting with the skin on.
