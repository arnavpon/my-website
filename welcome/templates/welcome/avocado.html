Avocado

Fruit, not vegetable. Excellent source of vitamin E, folate, potassium, magnesium, antioxidants, monounsaturated fat, soluble fiber (6 grams in 1 medium). Hass avocados are the highest in antioxidants.

  • Purchase - ripe when soft at the top but yielding only slightly in the middle (soft everywhere = over-ripe). For Hass avocados, the skin will turn nearly black when ripe.
  • Storage - can force ripen in paper bag (2-3 days) & speed process by adding a banana to the bag. Once ripe, store in the fridge for up to a day. To prevent an unused half from browning, keep the stone in, squirt with lemon juice, then store in airtight container or bag. Alternative is to store in container near chopped onions.
  • Preparation - excellent for salads b/c the fat increases BA of lutein & beta-carotene in the greens.
