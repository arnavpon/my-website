Asparagus

Low GI, high in antioxidants.

  • Purchase - look for dark green, shiny, straight spears with tightly closed tips of a green or purple color. The cut end of the stalk should be smooth & moist.
  • Storage - high respiration rate, so pick fresh & chill immediately.
  • Preparation - consume on the day of purchase. Steaming increases antioxidant value. Cooking is complete when the spear bends slightly when you hold it in the middle.
