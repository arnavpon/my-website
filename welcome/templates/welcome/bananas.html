Bananas & Plantains

Cavendish is variety of sweet banana sold in grocery stores (only one in most), is one of the least nutritious fruits available, & has a very high GI (riper = higher GI). Benefits are 3.5 grams soluble fiber & potassium (but it isn't in the top 10 richest sources). Eliminate from menu.

Foods that have more potassium than bananas - avocado, potato (regular & sweet), tomato, spinach & other greens, salmon, dried apricots, beans, dairy, squash (acorn).

  • Purchase - if you can find red bananas (turn black after ripening), try them instead.
