Artichoke

Extremely high in antioxidants, fiber (8-10 grams in 1 medium). Contains silymarin & cynarin which are protective for the liver. Also acts as a probiotic due to inulin.

  • Purchase - fresh artichoke is firm when squeezed.
  • Storage - b/c of a high respiration rate, they go bad quickly so buy fresh & eat within 2-3 days.
  • Preparation - cook by steaming (~50 minutes). First wash well, remove outer layer of leaves, and trim the stems to half an inch.
