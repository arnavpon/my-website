Apples

  • Purchase - choose uniformly bright red apples (color indicates sunlight exposure which correlates with nutrient content). Most nutritious varieties are Granny Smith, Honeycrisp, Gala, Fuji, & Cortland. Choose organic apples b/c skins are high in pesticide content (but also are the most nutritious part of the apple).
  • Storage - store in crisper drawer of fridge for maximum shelf life.
