Watermelon

Mostly water so low nutrient content. Red color comes from lycopene (dark red varieties are one of the best sources of lycopene).

  • Purchase - to select a ripe melon (best flavor & nutrition), find fruit that is beginning to lose its gloss; also, examine the ground spot (part in contact with ground), which should be yellow, not green or white. When you tap, listen for a hollow sound, not a dull thump.
  • Storage - after purchasing, keep on the counter (at room temperature) to increase nutrient content.
  • Preparation - serve chilled.
